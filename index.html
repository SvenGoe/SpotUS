About SpotUS

SpotUS' goal is to give all athletes the opportunity of pursuing a college career in the United States. We want to be the go-to place for those who cannont afford, or do not want to invest in an overpriced agency. As community we give guidance and support throughout the recruiting process, reducing some of the uncertainty that enables recruiting agencies to charge thousands of dollars for their service.
